Background of the Organization

Burnpur Commercial Institute was established in the year of 1977 at Subhaspally, Burnpur. with the view to trained the unemployed youth in the field of professional & vocational training in the Industrial Belt. That time, BCI taught the different types of commercial education, i.e. Typewriting, Shorthand, Stenography, Book-Keeping, modern English etc. And Affiliated to Board of Commercial Education, Govt. Of West Bengal under the Ministry of Higher Education till 2002. But after merger the Board of Commercial Education with the West Bengal State Council of Technical Education Govt. Of West Bengal with the modification some courses as per the demand of present Market as well as Industry scenario. At present BCI enjoying the Total No. of Six Affiliated Courses under the West Bengal State council of Technical Education Govt. Of West Bengal and also Affiliated State Govt. course PBSSD and NCVT.
